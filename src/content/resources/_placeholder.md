## Why Brand Infrastructure Matters

Most startups treat branding as an afterthought. The ones that don't raise more, hire better, and move faster.

## What to Build First

Start with these three foundations: visual identity, messaging framework, and a pitch deck that actually tells your story.

## When to Invest
